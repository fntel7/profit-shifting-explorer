Profit Shifting Explorer

Country-year and industry-year estimates of profit shifted by multinational enterprises, 2009–2020. Based on Delis, Delis, Laeven & Ongena, Journal of Accounting and Economics (2025).

2018
How to read this map. Pick a GUO country and a year. The map then shades each country by how much profit was shifted by MNEs whose Global Ultimate Owner sits in the chosen GUO country and whose lowest-tax jurisdiction is that shaded country. Shaded countries and the overlay bubbles are therefore lowest-tax jurisdictions, not the GUO country itself. Values are joint aggregates over the (GUO, lowest-tax country) combination — not bilateral flows. Small-island tax havens are marked with circles sized by value so they remain visible at world scale. Countries with no data for the combination are left light grey.
Yearly profit shifted by MNEs for each selected (GUO country · lowest-tax jurisdiction) combination. Pairs are ranked by all-time total across 2009–2020; the 20 largest are shown. Values are in billions of US dollars.
